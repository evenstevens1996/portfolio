About Us

We began this league 4 years ago. We started with 60 players our first seasonn and we now have over 400 this season. Here at Shelby's Soccer League, we strive to teach all players skills that they can use both on and off the field. We currently have leagues for kids 4 years old and up. One thing that is really unique about us, is that we also have an adult league. You and your family can register today!
