Under 6 Tots League

This group is between 4 and 6 years old! The Tots League will learn the basics while having some fun in the sun developing social skills while under performing in a team atmosphere. If registered for a singular season, ten games, pictures and jersey are included. Give your tots their first core memory and register today!

Under 8 Mini League

This group is between 6 and 8 years old! In the Mini League, they will learn the fundamentals of soccer by partipating in more structured games and practices. This is our youngest age group that features a year-round competitive soccer team. Also features a singular season league that is 4 months long and features 10 games. Pictures and uniform are included with registration! Help these minis sharpen their skills and join today!

Under 10 Mini Pro League

This league is from 8 to 10 years old! In the Mini Pro League, they will participate in more competitive games that will not only teach discipline, but the importance of preserverence. This age group features a year-round competitive league, as well as a seasonal league. The singular season league consists of 4 months and 12 games; jersey and photos are included. Give your mini pro a chance to sign up today!

Under 12 Pre-Teen League

This age group is from 10 to 12! In the Pre-Teen League, these players will learn from their experienced coaches the fine details of ball control and field positions. This age group features a year-round competitive league, as well as a seasonal league. The singular season league consists of 4 months and 12 games; jersey and photos are included. Give your pre-teen a new kind of outlet!

Under 16 Teen League

This league is from 12 to 16! In the Teen League, players will continue to sharpen their skills and begin to play a individualized position in the team and on the field. This age group features a year-round competitive league, as well as a seasonal league. The singular season league consists of 4 months and 16 games; jersey and photos are included. Give your teen a chance to sign up today!

Under 18 Young Adult League

This group is from 16 to 18! In the Young Adult League, games are at their most intense! Players have defined positions, as well as refined skills. This age group features a year-round competitive league, as well as a seasonal league. The singular season league consists of 4 months and 16 games; jersey and photos are included. Sign up today!

Adult Elder's League

Our Elder's League is for ages 18 and up. This is a year-round league that gives adults a break from their everyday routine. Registration includes team photos and uniform. Register today!
